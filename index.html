About

Introduction

With a robust academic background and extensive professional experience, Advocate Ashutosh Thakur is an accomplished Advocate on Record at the Supreme Court of India. Holding a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) from Banaras Hindu University and a Post Graduate Diploma in Intellectual Property Rights from the Indian Law Institute, Advocate Thakur has further honed his expertise through specialized courses, including a Certificate Course in Intellectual Property Rights from WIPO-UNIGE Summer School.

Professionally, Advocate Ashutosh Thakur has been serving as an Advocate on Record at the Supreme Court of India since September 2023. In addition, he is a Panel Advocate for Northern Coalfields Limited and an Amicus Curiae for the National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (NCDRC).

With over a decade of experience as a Legal Consultant for the Dental Council of India and a rich history as a Summer Trainee at the National Human Rights Commission (NHRC), Advocate Thakur brings a wealth of knowledge and a deep commitment to legal excellence in his practice.
